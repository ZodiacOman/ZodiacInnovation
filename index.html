Nizwa Stone

Natural Stone

Nizwa stone, also known as natural stone, is a traditional Omani building material prized for its durability, aesthetic appeal, and natural patterns. Widely used in construction and landscaping, it adds a timeless and authentic charm to both modern and heritage designs.

Stamped Concrete

United States

Stamped concrete is a decorative flooring option that mimics the appearance of natural materials like stone, brick, or wood by imprinting patterns and textures into freshly poured concrete. It is popular for its durability, cost-effectiveness, and ability to enhance the aesthetic appeal of patios, driveways, and walkways.

Laterite Stone

Kerala Stone

Laterite stone is a naturally occurring, reddish-brown building material formed from the weathering of rocks rich in iron and aluminum. Known for its durability, thermal insulation, and eco-friendliness, it is widely used in construction, especially in tropical regions.
